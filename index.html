Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is CodeReview AI free to use?
Yes — completely free. No account, no credit card, no limits beyond a short cooldown between reviews to keep the service running smoothly.
How accurate is the AI feedback?
The AI is powered by Claude (Anthropic) and reliably catches common issues like naming problems, logic bugs, and security risks. Always pair it with your own judgement.
What is Beginner Mode?
Beginner Mode rewrites all feedback in plain, encouraging language — no jargon, no assumed knowledge. Perfect if you're just learning HTML, CSS, or JavaScript.
Is my code stored or shared?
No. Your code is sent directly to the AI for analysis and is not stored or logged. Each review is stateless and independent.
Will more languages be added?
Yes. Python, TypeScript, and more are planned. The tool currently focuses on the three core web languages to keep the experience focused and high-quality.
